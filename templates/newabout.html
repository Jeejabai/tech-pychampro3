OUR COMPANY'S BACKGROUND

Industrial Company was established in 1990 as an Electro-Mechanical contracting company. It is well known in Saudi Arabia for engineering excellence, project completion dependability, quality of work, and after-sales service. It has adopted a division aliased organization structure with each division specializing in different business segments for better customer focus and providing the best possible service to achieve customer satisfaction. The contracting division is the flagship division of the company that encompasses both A/C HVAC contracting and general contracting. Additionally, to that, there is a trading division, which represents HVAC products & systems from different renowned companies including:

  • TACO, USA for chilled water pumps and hydraulic products.
  • BLUE STAR, India for central air-conditioning equipment & systems.
  • THERMOSCREEN, UK for Air curtains.
  • SYSTEMAIR, Germany for ventilation fans, etc.

The company strives to maintain the pioneer attributes and distinctions that it has gained at local and national levels. It also strives to expand its activities and investments, as well as raise its competitive capabilities and its human and material potential to become an international company with expanded activities and multi-national accreditation. In making this feasible, the company has made wide and strong relations with many regional and global economic institutions and unions.

Jala Industrial Company's activities permeate through various investment fields (Industrial, Trading, and Services). These activities spread in many cities. The most notable cities include Riyadh, Jeddah, Makkah, Taif, Yanbu, Gizan, and Najran. Over the years Jala Industrial Company has successfully completed over 200 projects of various sizes, in diverse applications from simple fit-out works to complicated projects such as universities, hospitals, shopping centers, and 5-star luxury hotels.
